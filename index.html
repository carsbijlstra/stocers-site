Good to know

Wood wool firelighters, explained.

What are wood wool firelighters?

Wood wool firelighters are made from fine, twisted wood wool dipped in natural wax. They are eco-friendly, neutral-scented and FSC certified, with nothing but wood and wax, so they are safe to use in ovens and grills.

How long does a STOCERS firelighter burn?

Each curl lights with a single match and burns a steady 8 to 10 minutes, long enough to get any fire going, without kindling or paper.

What can I light with them?

They ignite wood, briquettes, charcoal and eco-pellets, and they are ideal for fireplaces, wood stoves, BBQs, kamados, pizza ovens, chimineas, campfires and fire pits. An all-season product, useful all year round.

Yes. STOCERS is a producer and supplies by the full pallet from our own stock, with fast delivery across Europe. We also produce private label per truck load. Become a dealer to request wholesale terms.
